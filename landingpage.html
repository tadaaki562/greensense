What Is GreenSense?

GreenSense is a smart platform designed to help small greenhouse farmers turn everyday transactions into valuable insights. Every time a farmer buys seeds, fertilizers, or soil, or sells crops to a local market, restaurant, or subscription box, GreenSense collects and analyzes the information.

But it's not just about recording sales and expenses. GreenSense uses intelligent analysis to answer questions like:

  • Which crops are making the most money?
  • Where am I spending too much?
  • How much should I plant next season?
  • Which customers or markets are the most profitable?

Why Farmers Need GreenSense

Small greenhouse farmers face common problems that GreenSense helps solve:

  • Financial Blind Spots Many small farmers don't know the real cost of producing each crop. Seeds, soil, fertilizer, water, and labor all add up, but without a clear picture, farmers may not realize which crops are actually profitable.
  • Wasted Produce When farmers don't have insight into which crops sell best, they risk overplanting. Unsold or spoiled crops mean lost money.
  • Cash Flow Challenges Sometimes buyers take longer to pay, or suppliers' prices change unexpectedly. Farmers need to know which customers or suppliers are reliable.
  • Planning Difficulties Knowing how much to plant next season or how to adjust crop selection based on demand can be tricky without good data.

GreenSense Solves These Problems

It gives farmers the tools they need to make smart decisions, reduce waste, and grow more profitably.
